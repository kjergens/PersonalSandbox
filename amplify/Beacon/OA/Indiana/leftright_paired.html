Animals - A Rhyming Poem
  1. Animals live in many places - in water, grass, and trees.
  2. Some hop, some jump, some climb, while others run with ease.
  3. Animals can swim in lakes or fly high in the sky.
  4. Smaller ones crawl in the rocks for a shady place to lie.
  5. Whales dive deep to look for food in an ocean or a sea.
  6. And bees buzz among the flowers, gathering nectar happily.
  7. If I could be an animal - perhaps a deer or bear, I would live wild in the woods and run from here to there.
  8. The End
