About me

UX Designer with a background in software engineering. Specialize in interaction design but also fluent in information architecture and data visualization.

I love teaching, coaching, and mentoring computer science students. In 2014-15 I mentored a Girls Who Code club and Technovation Challenge high school team. I currently teach computer science and user experience part-time at the prestigious Dalton School here in New York City.

I ran the Dublin Marathon in 2012.
