About Me

User Experience Design professional specializing in interaction design, information architecture and data visualization.

I was a Software Engineer for 6 years, an Automation Engineer / QA Manager for 8 years and, most recently, a UX designer for the past 4 years.

I focus on working with the whole team - from Sales, to Dev to QA - on how Design fits into the software life cycle. I want to design good products but also good processes for delivering them and evaluating their success.

I love teaching and mentoring computer science students. In 2013-14, I instructed a Girls Who Code club and mentored a Samsung Technovation Challenge team.

I currently teach Computer Science at The Dalton School.
