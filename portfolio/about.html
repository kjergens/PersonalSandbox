About Me

User Experience Design professional specializing in interaction design, information architecture and data visualization.

In 2004, I moved to New York from Seattle as a Software Engineer. From 2004-2010, I worked as an Automation Engineer and QA Manager while studying design. I've been full-time a UX designer since 2010.

I love teaching and mentoring beginning computer scientists. In 2013-14, I instructed a Girls Who Code club and mentored a Samsung Technovation Challenge team.

I currently teach Computer Science at The Dalton School.
