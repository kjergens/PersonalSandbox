Create The Perfect Color Scheme With This Simple Interactive Tool (Part 1)

Do these colors 'go'? For your webpage, logo or bedroom, you might be scared your color choices don't pass graphics muster. I say if it feels good do it, but for you perfectionists here's a way to get a fail-proof color palette. We'll start big: with a 4-color scheme.

First, find a colorwheel. The more hues the better. Then pick a color you like.

You're almost done! To get the 3 remaining colors, decide on one of 2 color schemes: tetradic (vibrant, contrasting) or analogous (similar, coherent).
