How To Make A Color Scheme, Pt 1 of 2

For your webpage, logo or bedroom, you might be scared your color choices don't pass graphics muster. I say if it feels good do it, but for you perfectionists here are steps to a fail-proof color palette. We'll start big: with a 4-color scheme.

First, find a colorwheel like the one below. The more hues the better. Then pick a color you like.

You're almost done! To get the 3 remaining colors, decide on one of 2 color schemes: tetradic (vibrant, contrasting) or analogous (similar, coherent).
