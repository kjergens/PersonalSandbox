Color Wheel For Coffee

Want to describe your coffee like a pro? You're in luck! The Specialty Coffee Association of America updated it's Coffee Taster's Flavor Wheel for the first time since the original in 1998.

It goes well beyond the basics of sweet, sour, bitter and salty, inviting you to describe coffee as papery, vegatative, smoky or caramelized.

The colors in the new wheel align well with the flavors they represent and the negative space allow them to breathe. Good improvements to the comparatively compact original (below).

Now let's see...One half-caff papery latte with a lime aftertaste, please!
